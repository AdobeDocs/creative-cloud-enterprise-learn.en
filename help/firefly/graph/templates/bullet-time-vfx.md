# Bullet Time VFX

Learn how to feed a hero product or subject image to generate a rotating sequence of angles around it, then stitch the freeze frame sweep automatically. [Open Bullet Time VFX template](https://firefly.adobe.com/graph/edit/id/urn:aaid:sc:US:3efb4f51-345d-58eb-b7c8-7b289f0dd1c4).

>[!TIP]
>
>**Before you start** - For the best results customize this template to your own brand, product, and workflow. Swap in your reference images, prompts, and copy before using any output.

[!BADGE Use cases]{type=Informative tooltip="Use cases"}

* **Outdoors** - Build a bullet-time hero shot of a climber in motion for a paid social ad, without a multi-camera rig on location.
* **Retail** - Create a 360-degree freeze-frame shot of a new sneaker for a product launch page.
* **Automotive** - Generate a rotating hero shot of a vehicle for a digital showroom, without a turntable studio session.
